About me

I am a sixth year Ph.D. student in Stony Brook University. My advisor is Radu Laza. In Fall 2021, I am on the postdoc job market.

Sometimes I write my name as Yoonjoo Kim without a dash.

My field of interest is algebraic geometry, especially on topics related to compact hyper-Kähler manifolds.
